About me

Ksenia Baiduraeva

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Ksenia. I was born in 1991 in Ulyanovsk where I grew up and finished my studies, graduating from the Ulyanovsk State University with a degree in acting. Right after that I got accepted to the Ulyanovsk Theater of Drama.

Ater working for three years as a leading actress in Ulyanovsk Theater of Drama, I decided to travel and learn more about the world - I went to China where I worked as an English teacher. One year later I returned to Russia and moved to Saint-Petersburg to open new professional boundaries.

For the next three years I worked at the Levendal Theatre, where I was lucky enough to try out completely different roles, and not just as an actor but even as a director and writer. In this theatre I finally made the transition from playing young romantic heroines to acting as mature and powerful heroines.

When I younger, I had been dancing folk dance for almost 10 year; now I sing, practice yoga, do a lot of handicrafts, plant flowers, and recently I have mastered a completely new profession - a software tester and started to work in an IT company.

All my life I have been discovering and developing myself and now I also don't stop to discover and search, search for interesting projects. I have a tenacious mind and a sensitive heart, which has always helped me in my work and in building up my roles. I am sure that our collaboration will be a successful and fruitful for each of us.
